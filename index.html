BECAfest is the annual showcase of student work produced in Broadcast and Electronic Communication Arts. Emulating Hollywood award shows, BECAfest includes a yearly theme (a guarded secret until the show), music, dynamic hosts and the award-winning work. Entries are judged in categories as diverse as our students, including video, audio, screenwriting, radio, sound art and experimental video. Winners jump on stage to receive the coveted Becky Award and make their acceptance speeches.
